Pancakes

Easy and fluffy pancakes perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Method

  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add milk and egg, then mix well.
  3. Heat a pan and pour some batter.
  4. Cook both sides until golden brown.
  5. Serve and enjoy.
